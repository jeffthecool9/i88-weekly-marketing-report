Challenges & Solutions

7 IDENTIFIED
High Risk

Ad Account Restriction

Facebook may restrict or disable the ad account at any time — policy flags, unusual spend spikes, or automated reviews. A single account ban halts all 6 active campaigns and kills revenue flow instantly.

IMPACT: 100% downtime on all campaigns if primary account gets banned
Solution

Buy More Ad Accounts for Risk Coverage

  • Purchase 3–5 additional ad accounts via separate verified Business Manager profiles to distribute risk
  • Split by market — MY campaigns on Account A & B, SG campaigns on Account C & D
  • Warm up new accounts slowly — start RM30/day, increase 20% every 3 days to avoid triggering flags
  • Mirror winning campaigns on backup accounts — if primary goes down, activate backup within 30 minutes
  • Never run all spend from one BM — diversify ownership across 2–3 Business Managers
Revenue Loss

MY Welcome Bonus (CN) Below 1.0x ROAS

Campaign dropped to 0.74x ROAS (-34% vs last week). Spent RM720 but generated only RM532 in value. The retarget audience is likely exhausted — frequency is climbing and conversions are dropping fast.

IMPACT: RM188 net loss this week, bleeding since Week 3
Solution

Pause, Refresh, Restructure

  • Keep paused for 7 days minimum — let audience pool refresh and reduce ad fatigue
  • Create new creatives in Chinese — video testimonials of real winners, not just static bonus banners
  • Switch audience — move from retarget to Lookalike 1% of past depositors instead
  • Reallocate RM720 budget to MY Slots Promo (3.12x ROAS) — proven winner gets more fuel
  • Set kill rule — any campaign below 1.0x ROAS after RM500 spend auto-pauses
Declining

MY Sports Betting Down -8% Week-over-Week

Sports Betting campaign ROAS fell from 2.54x to 2.34x. Cost per result crept up to RM25. Broad targeting is running out of cheap inventory — the easy conversions have been captured, and Meta is now reaching less qualified users.

IMPACT: Will drop below 2.0x within 1-2 weeks if not addressed
Solution

Audience Refresh + World Cup Angle

  • Build Lookalike audience from existing Sports Betting converters — 1% LAL will be more targeted than broad
  • World Cup 2026 creative angle — use match schedules, live odds, and "Bet on Malaysia" hooks in BM copy
  • Test video format — screen recording of live betting interface showing a winning bet with payout
  • Exclude converted users — stop paying to reach people who already registered
Expensive

SG Slots Promo High Cost Per Result

SG Slots campaign is paying RM31.61 per result — 40% higher than the MY equivalent (RM22.54). Singapore's ad auction is more competitive, but the gap is too wide to sustain at scale.

IMPACT: RM9.07 overpay per result vs MY benchmark = RM281 wasted this week
Solution

SG-Specific Creative + SGD Pricing

  • Create SG-specific ad copy — reference SGD amounts, Singapore payment methods (PayNow, GrabPay)
  • Use Singlish-flavoured English in copy — "Shiok bonus waiting for you" resonates more than generic English
  • Test WhatsApp over Messenger — SG market prefers WhatsApp for business conversations
  • Narrow age range — SG sweet spot is 25–44, exclude below 21 and above 55 to reduce waste
Fatigue

Creative Fatigue Across Campaigns

Most campaigns have been running the same 2–3 creatives for 3+ weeks. Frequency is climbing above 2.5 on MY campaigns. When users see the same ad repeatedly, CTR drops, CPR spikes, and ROAS erodes — even on winning audiences.

IMPACT: Estimated 15-20% ROAS decay if creatives are not refreshed within 7 days
Solution

Creative Refresh Pipeline

  • Produce 5 new creatives per week — mix of video (15s), carousel, and static with bold Malay/English headlines
  • Rotate every 7–10 days — turn off any creative with frequency > 3.0, replace with fresh variant
  • Use UGC-style content — screen recordings, "real player" testimonials, phone-filmed reactions to wins
  • A/B test hooks — first 3 seconds of video determines 80% of performance, test 3 different hooks per creative
Tracking

Incomplete Conversion Tracking

Registrations from WhatsApp conversations are not fully tracked back to Meta. Some deposits show as organic when they came from ads. Without complete data, Meta's algorithm can't optimize for the right outcome — ROAS numbers are likely underreported.

IMPACT: True ROAS may be 20-30% higher but Meta can't learn from it
Solution

Full-Funnel Attribution Setup

  • Implement Conversions API (CAPI) — server-side tracking that works even with iOS privacy restrictions
  • Upload offline conversions weekly — export deposits from backend, match to Meta click IDs, feed back to ad account
  • Tag every link with UTM parameters — utm_source=facebook&utm_campaign=[name]&utm_content=[creative_id]
  • Aim for Event Match Quality > 6.0 in Meta Events Manager for accurate attribution
Conversion

Landing Page to Registration Drop-Off

Estimated 35–40% of clicks from ads do not complete registration. Users land on the page but bounce before signing up. The gap between ad click and registration is where the most budget is wasted — every lost click is a paid opportunity gone.

IMPACT: ~140 potential registrations lost this week (RM3,400 in wasted clicks)
Solution

Landing Page Optimization

  • Reduce page load to under 3 seconds — compress images, lazy load below-fold content, use CDN
  • Match ad message to landing page — if ad says "100% Welcome Bonus", hero headline must say the same
  • Simplify registration form — phone number + password only, no unnecessary fields
  • Add trust signals above the fold — "Beroperasi sejak 2016", "Withdraw 5 minit", "24/7 Support"
  • A/B test 2 landing page variants — video hero vs static hero, measure registration rate difference

Advanced Optimization Structure

4 PHASES
01

Foundation & Infrastructure

Fix tracking and protect accounts

This Week

Multi-Account Setup

Provision 3–5 verified ad accounts across 2–3 Business Managers. Assign MY to Account A+B, SG to Account C+D. Warm each account at RM30/day for 7 days before scaling.

Tracking Pipeline

Install Conversions API + offline conversion uploads. Every registration and deposit must be tracked back to Meta. Target Event Match Quality > 6.0 for each ad account.

Naming Convention

Standardize: [MARKET]_[PRODUCT]_[LANG]_[AUDIENCE]_[DATE]. Example: MY_Slots_BM_LAL1%-Depositors_Jun26. Enables instant filtering and cross-account analysis.

Automated Rules

Set rules: Pause if ROAS < 1.0x after RM500. Increase budget 15% if ROAS > 3.0x for 3 days. Alert if frequency > 3.0. Alert if daily spend drops 30% unexpectedly (account issue signal).

02

Creative & Audience Testing

Systematic discovery of winners

Week 5–6

Creative Matrix

Test 3 hooks x 3 formats x 2 languages = 18 variants. Formats: video (15s), carousel, static. Languages: BM natural copy, English, Chinese. Kill any variant below 1.5x ROAS after RM200.

Audience Layers

Layer 1: LAL 1% from depositors (highest value). Layer 2: LAL 2% from registrations (volume). Layer 3: Interest-based (casino, sports). Layer 4: Broad (let Meta find). Test independently at RM50/day ABO.

Market-Specific Copy

MY-BM: "Daftar Sekarang & Claim Bonus 100%! Withdraw 5 minit." SG-EN: "Claim Your Welcome Bonus — SGD deposits, instant withdrawal." Match tone to market, never generic copy across regions.

Landing Page A/B

Test 2 variants per market: Variant A = video hero + single CTA. Variant B = image hero + benefit bullets + CTA. Track registration rate. Winner gets all traffic by end of Week 6.

03

Scaling Winners

Amplify proven campaigns systematically

Week 6–8

Vertical Scaling

For campaigns above 2.5x ROAS: increase budget 20% every 3 days. Never double overnight. MY Slots (3.12x) scales first: RM2,840 → RM3,400 → RM4,100. Monitor ROAS daily — pause scaling if it drops below 2.0x.

Horizontal Scaling

Duplicate winning ad sets to new audiences. Clone SG Live Casino (3.65x ROAS) creative into MY market with BM copy. Clone MY Slots creative into SG with SGD pricing. Cross-pollinate proven assets.

Budget Framework

70/20/10 rule: 70% of budget to proven winners (ROAS > 2.5x), 20% to active testing, 10% to experimental new markets/products. Current RM8,420 → target RM12,000/week by Week 8.

Cross-Account Distribution

As spend scales past RM15,000/week, split campaigns across ad accounts. No single account should carry > RM8,000/week. This protects against restriction risk and maintains delivery stability.

04

Retention & Expansion

Maximize LTV + new channels

Week 8+

Retargeting Funnel

Tier 1: Clicked ad but didn't register — show social proof (player count, withdrawal speed). Tier 2: Registered but didn't deposit — show first-deposit bonus urgency. Tier 3: Past depositors — upsell new games/promos. RM30–50/day per tier.

WhatsApp Response SOP

Reply within 5 minutes — response time directly impacts conversion. Script: greeting → qualifying question → bonus explanation → registration link → deposit guidance. Track conversation-to-deposit rate per agent.

Platform Expansion

Once Facebook hits 3.0x blended ROAS consistently, test TikTok Ads (younger demographic, video-native) and Google Ads (search intent for "online casino Malaysia"). Allocate 15% of total budget to new channels.

Weekly Review Ritual

Every Monday: Review spend, ROAS, CPR across all accounts. Kill losers (< 1.0x after RM500), scale winners (+20%), launch 2–3 new test ad sets. Update this report. Track progress against 2.5x blended target.
