Testing

Find new winning ads & audiences

Week 5–6
  • New creatives — test different hooks, formats, and languages.
  • New audiences — test Lookalike, interest, and broad targeting.
  • Local copy — write unique ad copy per market, no generic translations.
  • Landing page test — run 2 designs, keep the better-converting one.
03

Scaling

Grow what's already working

Week 6–8
  • Raise budgets slowly — +20% every 3 days on campaigns above 2.5x ROAS.
  • Copy winners — bring top ads into new markets and audiences.
  • 70/20/10 budget split — winners / testing / new ideas.
  • Spread spend — split across accounts once weekly spend passes RM15,000.

Retention

Keep customers, explore new channels

Week 8+

  • Fast replies — answer WhatsApp messages within 5 minutes.
  • New platforms — test TikTok & Google once Facebook hits 3.0x ROAS.
  • Weekly check-in — review numbers every Monday, adjust fast.
