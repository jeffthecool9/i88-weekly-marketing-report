Challenges & Fixes

6 ITEMS
01

Ad Account Restriction

High Risk

Problem:Facebook can ban our account anytime — all 9 campaigns would stop instantly.

Fix:Buy 3–5 backup ad accounts and mirror our live campaigns on them.

02

Duplicate Campaign Names

Confusing

Problem:4 campaigns share near-identical names (two "Jersey (BM)", two "+RM150") — impossible to tell apart in reports.

Fix:Add a unique tag (audience or date) to every campaign name from now on.

03

Most Spend Has No Registration Data

Data Gap

Problem:5 of 9 campaigns (RM656 spend) show zero tracked leads — we can't tell if they're working.

Fix:Add lead tracking to every active campaign, not just some.

04

Two Dead Campaigns Still Listed

Cleanup Needed

Problem:One archived (RM0.47) and one inactive (RM62.72) campaign are still cluttering the report.

Fix:Remove dead campaigns from reporting and reallocate their budget.

05

BM Creative Converts Best

Opportunity

Problem:The Chinese-language ad costs RM218.88 per lead — over 4x the Bahasa Malaysia ad at RM50.28.

Fix:Shift more budget into the BM creative — it's the proven winner.

06

High Cost Per Registration

Expensive

Problem:Blended cost is RM147.46 per registration — too high for a traffic campaign.

Fix:Tighten targeting and add a clearer registration call-to-action.

Optimization Plan

4 PHASES
01

Foundation

Fix tracking, protect accounts

This Week
  • Backup accounts — set up 3–5 to avoid losing all campaigns to a ban.
  • Fix tracking — install Conversions API for accurate registration data.
  • Standard naming — name campaigns consistently for easy reporting.
  • Auto-rules — auto-pause losers, auto-scale winners.
02

Testing

Find new winning ads & audiences

Week 5–6
  • New creatives — test different hooks, formats, and languages.
  • New audiences — test Lookalike, interest, and broad targeting.
  • Local copy — write unique ad copy per market, no generic translations.
  • Landing page test — run 2 designs, keep the better-converting one.
03

Scaling

Grow what's already working

Week 6–8
  • Shift budget to BM — move spend from the weak CN ad into the proven BM creative.
  • Copy winners — bring top ads into new markets and audiences.
  • 70/20/10 budget split — winners / testing / new ideas.
  • Spread spend — split across accounts as weekly budget grows.
04

Retention

Keep customers, explore new channels

Week 8+
  • Retarget drop-offs — re-engage people who clicked but didn't convert.
  • Fast replies — answer WhatsApp messages within 5 minutes.
  • New platforms — test TikTok & Google once registration tracking is reliable.
  • Weekly check-in — review numbers every Monday, adjust fast.
